Terms and Conditions for Food Providers
1. Food Safety and Quality

Providers must ensure all donated food items are safe for consumption and meet local health department standards.

2. Accurate Information

All information provided about food items must be accurate, including expiry dates, quantities, and preparation methods.

3. Availability and Pickup

Providers must honor confirmed pickup arrangements and update availability status promptly.

4. Liability

Providers are responsible for ensuring food safety until pickup. The platform facilitates connections but does not assume liability.

5. Non-Discrimination

Food donations must be offered without discrimination based on race, religion, gender, or other protected characteristics.
